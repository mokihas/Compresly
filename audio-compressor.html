How to Compress Audio

  1. Upload Your Audio File: Drag and drop your audio file into the upload area, or click to select it from your computer.
  2. Select Compression Level: Use the slider to choose your desired compression level. Higher compression reduces file size but may slightly decrease quality.
  3. Choose Output Format: Select the desired output format (MP3, WAV, OGG).
  4. Compress: Click the "Compress Audio" button to start the compression process.
  5. Download: Once the compression is complete, click the "Download Audio" button to save the compressed file.
