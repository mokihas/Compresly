Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We never upload your files to our servers. All processing happens locally in your browser.

What file types can I compress?

We support JPG, PNG, WebP images, PDF documents, and MP4 videos. More formats coming soon!

Are there any file size limits?

Current limits are 10MB for images, 25MB for PDFs, and 50MB for videos. We're working to increase these.

How is this service free?

We're supported by non-intrusive ads. You can optionally donate to help keep us running.
