How Compresly Works

Our 3-step process makes file optimization simple and secure

1

Select Your Tool

Choose the appropriate compressor for your file type. We support images (JPG, PNG, WebP), PDF documents, videos (MP4, MOV), and audio files (MP3, WAV). Each tool is specially optimized for that file type.

Download & Use

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Compresly

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely! Unlike many online tools, Compresly processes all files directly in your web browser. This means:

  • Your files never upload to any server
  • No one else can access your documents
  • Files are automatically deleted after processing
  • We use industry-standard encryption for all transfers

What file types and sizes can I compress?

We support a wide range of formats with generous size limits:

Images:

JPG, PNG, WebP (up to 15MB)

Documents:

PDF (up to 30MB)

Videos:

MP4, MOV (up to 100MB)

Audio:

MP3, WAV (up to 50MB)

Will compression reduce my file quality?

Our smart algorithms minimize quality loss while maximizing size reduction:

  • Images: Adjustable quality settings let you balance size vs quality
  • PDFs: Text remains sharp while optimizing embedded images
  • Videos: Preserves resolution while reducing bitrate
  • Audio: Maintains clarity while optimizing file size

How is this service free?

Compresly is supported by non-intrusive advertisements. You can help us by:

  • Whitelisting us in your ad blocker
  • Sharing our tool with others
  • Donating to support development

We never charge for basic compression features.

Do you store my files after compression?

No. Compresly was designed with privacy in mind:

  • All processing happens in your browser
  • Files are deleted immediately after processing
  • We don't have access to your original or compressed files
  • No account registration required
