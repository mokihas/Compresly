Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2024

1. Information We Process

When you use Compresly, we may handle the following data:

  • Uploaded Files: All processing occurs in your browser. We do not store your files permanently - they are deleted immediately after processing.
  • Technical Data: Anonymized IP address, browser type, device type, and country-level location for analytics.
  • Usage Data: Compression settings selected, tool usage frequency, and error reports (no personal identification).

2. Data Usage

We use data exclusively to:

  • Provide and improve our compression tools
  • Monitor service performance and prevent abuse
  • Develop new features based on aggregated usage patterns
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google AdSense: Serves personalized ads using cookies. Opt-out via Google's Ad Settings.
  • Google Analytics: Tracks anonymized usage data with IP anonymization enabled.

We never sell your data to third parties.

4. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for:

  • Ad personalization (via AdSense)
  • Remembering user preferences (e.g., dark mode)
  • Preventing fraud

Manage cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

Security measures include:

  • Client-side file processing (no server uploads)
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Policy Changes

Updates will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
