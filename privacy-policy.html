Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use ImageCompressorPro, we may process the following data:

  • Uploaded Images: All image processing occurs directly in your browser. We do not permanently store, transmit, or retain copies of your uploaded images on our servers after processing is complete.
  • Technical Data: Including but not limited to IP address (anonymized), browser type and version, device characteristics, operating system, and approximate geographic location (country-level).
  • Usage Analytics: Non-personal data about your interactions with our service, including compression settings selected, time spent on page, and error logs for debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We utilize collected data exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain our free image compression service
  • Develop new features and improve existing functionality
  • Monitor service performance and diagnose technical issues
  • Prevent fraudulent or abusive usage of our platform
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize Google AdSense for advertising, which employs cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing behavior. You may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings. Our service also uses Google Analytics to understand traffic patterns, which may collect anonymized usage data.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • All image processing occurs client-side in your browser
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits of our infrastructure

While we strive to protect your data, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We recommend avoiding uploading sensitive or confidential images.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via prominent service announcements. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
