How to Compress PDF

  1. Upload Your PDF: Drag and drop your PDF into the upload area, or click to select it from your computer.
  2. Select Compression Level: Use the slider to choose your desired compression level. Higher compression reduces file size but may slightly decrease quality.
  3. Compress: Click the "Compress PDF" button to start the compression process.
  4. Download: Once the compression is complete, click the "Download PDF" button to save the compressed file.
