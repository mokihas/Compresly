About Compresly

Simplifying File Compression Since 2024

Our Story

Compresly was born out of frustration with bloated file sizes and complicated compression tools. In 2024, our team set out to create the simplest, fastest way to compress files without sacrificing quality - completely free and accessible to everyone.

Why Choose Compresly?

Privacy First

All files are processed in your browser - nothing is uploaded to our servers. Your files stay 100% private.

No Limits

Compress as many files as you need, with no watermarks, registration, or hidden fees.

Cutting-Edge Tech

We use advanced compression algorithms to maximize quality while minimizing file size.
